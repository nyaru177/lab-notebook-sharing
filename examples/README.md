# 每周示例 Notebook

这里放根据《实验室新生暑期居家集训学习计划》整理的示例 Notebook。每周一个，目的是让新提交者直接看到一份完整的“长什么样”，包括文件命名、作者和来源信息、学习目标、环境说明、Markdown 解释、代码和结果分析。

示例只抽取《实验室新生暑期居家集训学习计划》中的一部分内容，不是完整周作业，也不限制大家的选题。实际提交时可以参考对应周次的结构，再把自己的主题和实验替换进去。

| 周次 | 示例 | 示例重点 |
|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | [`week1/week1_visualization_example.ipynb`](week1/week1_visualization_example.ipynb) | 训练曲线、混淆矩阵、ROC、t-SNE 等可视化 |
| Week 2 | [`week2/week2_sklearn_pytorch_example.ipynb`](week2/week2_sklearn_pytorch_example.ipynb) | Pipeline、GridSearchCV、autograd |
| Week 3 | [`week3/week3_text_image_example.ipynb`](week3/week3_text_image_example.ipynb) | TF-IDF、t-SNE、RGB/BGR、边缘检测 |
| Week 4 | [`week4/week4_bilstm_resnet_example.ipynb`](week4/week4_bilstm_resnet_example.ipynb) | BiLSTM 前向传播、ResNet hook |
| Week 5 | [`week5/week5_content_safety_metrics_example.ipynb`](week5/week5_content_safety_metrics_example.ipynb) | Recall、Precision、F1 与阈值选择 |
| Week 6 | [`week6/week6_paper_reading_example.ipynb`](week6/week6_paper_reading_example.ipynb) | 论文精读记录结构 |
| Week 7 | [`week7/week7_reproduction_scaffold_example.ipynb`](week7/week7_reproduction_scaffold_example.ipynb) | baseline、随机种子、消融记录 |
| Week 8 | [`week8/week8_optimization_example.ipynb`](week8/week8_optimization_example.ipynb) | 固定条件下的优化比较 |

真实提交文件建议放到 `01-jupyter/` 到 `08-git-linux/` 或 `optional/` 中最匹配的分类目录，文件名使用：

```text
weekX_topic_name.ipynb
```

示例文件名中的 `_example` 只是为了和同学自己的提交区分。
